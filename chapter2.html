CHAPTER TWO

The Scholar in the Shadows

Matthias Reyes lived among books, archives, and forgotten knowledge hidden beneath the movement of modern city life.

Days passed between shelves and records inside the city library. Nights carried him somewhere different. Above the library sat a small loft untouched by noise and distraction.

Old lamps cast light across scattered manuscripts and worn pages. Ancient writings covered tables and floors. Dust drifted through still air. Silence filled the room.

Matthias searched for truth. Not information. Not theories. Truth.

One evening he opened a manuscript and read words that suddenly felt alive.

"The kingdom of heaven suffers violence, and the violent take it by force."

The room changed. Pressure filled the air. Movement stirred beyond what eyes could see. Something had noticed him. Something holy. Something powerful.

Fear rose for a moment. Matthias whispered one name. Jesus.

Warmth surged through him. Clarity replaced uncertainty. Knowledge suddenly felt incomplete without action.

He understood one truth with absolute certainty. Heaven was moving.

The city outside continued without noticing. Matthias stood and stepped toward the night knowing his life had entered something larger than study.
