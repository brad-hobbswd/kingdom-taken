# The Kingdom Taken

A cinematic Christian fiction experience by Bradley Hobbs.

Step into a modern city where unseen spiritual conflict unfolds beneath ordinary life. Three men from different backgrounds begin sensing a holy stirring that pulls them into a reality larger than themselves. Streets become battlegrounds. Faith becomes action. Heaven presses into the present.

## Live Site

Visit the experience here:

## About The Story

The Kingdom Taken follows:

Elias Vargas  
The quiet listener who has spent years learning to hear God beneath modern noise.

Matthias Reyes  
A scholar searching ancient truth who discovers knowledge alone cannot transform lives.

Abram Torres  
A watchman who begins recognizing battles hidden beneath city streets.

Separate paths become one journey as spiritual pressure rises and a Kingdom movement begins.

Inspired by Matthew 11:12:

"The kingdom of heaven suffers violence, and the violent take it by force."
