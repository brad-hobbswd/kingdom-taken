THE KINGDOM TAKEN

THE VIOLENT TAKE IT BY FORCE

By Bradley Hobbs

Chapter One

The Quiet Listener

Elias Vargas restores forgotten things by day and listens for God beneath the noise of modern life. A strange stirring begins changing everything.

Chapter Two

The Scholar in the Shadows

Matthias Reyes discovers that ancient truth carries a present call. Hidden revelation begins shaking his world.

Chapter Three

The Watchman in the City

Abram Torres watches over city streets while sensing battles invisible to everyone around him.

Chapter Four

The Meeting in the Ruins

Three strangers drawn by one spiritual force discover a connection larger than coincidence.

Chapter Five

The Pressing of Darkness

Spiritual resistance intensifies. Pressure builds. Faith becomes more than words.

Chapter Six

The Seizing of the Kingdom

The city becomes a battlefield as ordinary men step into extraordinary purpose.

"The kingdom of heaven suffers violence, and the violent take it by force."
Matthew 11:12
