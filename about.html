Bradley Hobbs

Bradley Hobbs writes stories and faith centered works that explore hidden struggles, spiritual journeys, restoration, and hope. His writing often blends everyday life with deeper realities that challenge readers toward courage and purpose. The Kingdom Taken explores unseen battles taking place beneath ordinary life and follows people discovering that heaven still moves among modern streets.

The Vision

Stories can reveal truths that everyday conversations sometimes miss. Each page seeks to point readers toward faith, perseverance, and spiritual awareness.

Current Work

Bradley continues building stories and devotion centered projects that encourage readers through honesty, hope, and encounters with Jesus.
